Title: #100DaysOfHustle Logo

Creator(s): Jess Lord, Leanne Jones

Brief:

To create and animate a logo for the #100DaysOfHustle project

Result and Reflection:

The logo, created by Leanne, was inspired by time, in particular the solar system. Jess kept this theme in mind whilst creating the animation; using rotations to reflect the orbiting of planets whilst revealing the text.

I really enjoyed animating the logo as it let me test my skills on AfterEffects which is something I am learning. It was great to collaborate with a fellow designer and combine our skills. - Jess
