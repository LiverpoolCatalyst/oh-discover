Title: OH Catalyst Snapchat Filters

Creator(s): Ross Hetherington

Brief:

Produce a set of Snapchat filters to provide a fun way for everyone to add the OH/Catalyst brand on Graduation day.

  1. “OH is” Filter - for everyone to take selfies with to provide a bank of graduate selfies for Robyn to post on Instagram etc.

  2. “Catalyst Graduation’ Filter - something fun for everyone to use while Snapping away on the night

  3. “OH Snap, Innovators Hub is now OH!” Filter - to increase awareness of new ‘OH’ brand after it has been announced on the night.

Result and Reflection:

Ross was able to use the experience he has built up over the past year of working with clients to produce branded Snapchat filters for events & businesses.

The filters were created to provide a fun way for people to capture the Graduation day and encourage them to add the OH/Catalyst branding before sharing.

Also produced a poster to be hung up around the venue to draw attention to the filters and encourage use.
