Title: Catalyst Experience Mobile Video

Subtitle: Vertical mobile video capturing the Catalyst experience from the perspective of an iPhone

Creator(s): Ross Hetherington, Jade Janeen

Brief:

Capture footage & create a video to showcase the Catalyst experience.

Result and Reflection:

This was a great project to learn from having never used any video editing software before!

We were able to learn the basics of Adobe Premiere Pro through trial and error and pretty happy with the end result given that it was a first attempt!

Can hopefully build on the skills learnt to learn some more advanced editing techniques in the future and take advantage of the growing trend in vertical mobile video.
