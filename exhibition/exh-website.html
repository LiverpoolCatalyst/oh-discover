Title: OH Discover Website

Subtitle: n/a

Creator(s): Amy Collin, Muge Alev

Brief:

Create a website to support the OH Discover Exhibition during and after the event to act as a platform for connecting the students and the guests.

Result and Reflection:

The website evolved many times during the development phase, influenced by changes to the event itself as well as the nature of the exhibition.

The present layout was initially designed by Amy and can be seen in the wireframes linked below. During the ideation phase, Muge also developed a set of wireframes, linked below.

The design was inspired by a child like sense of discovery and curiosity, for example the playful patterned banner and colourful backgrounds on each page. This echoes the discovering of new life and career opportunities in the creative digital sector for Catalyst participants.

The website is built using Bootstrap to utilise it's responsive features and speed of building page components. The most time consuming feature was to create the grid of headshots on the people page. The on:hover feature was implemented using jQuery.
